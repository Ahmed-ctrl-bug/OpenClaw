Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our treatments and clinic.

Most patients require 6-8 sessions spaced 4-6 weeks apart for optimal results. The exact number depends on the treatment area, hair type, and skin tone. During your consultation, we'll create a personalized treatment plan.
Hair transplant procedures are performed under local anesthesia, so you won't feel pain during the procedure. Post-operative discomfort is minimal and manageable with prescribed medication. Most patients return to normal activities within a few days.
For best results, we recommend a Hydra Facial every 4-6 weeks. This allows your skin to complete its natural renewal cycle between treatments. Some patients opt for monthly sessions as part of their regular skincare routine.
