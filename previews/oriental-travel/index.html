Paradise on Earth

Kashmir Valley

Glide across the mirror-still waters of Dal Lake on a hand-carved shikara at sunrise. Wander through the terraced Mughal Gardens of Nishat & Shalimar. Explore the saffron fields of Pampore and the old city streets of Srinagar. Stay in a heritage houseboat and wake to the sound of kingfishers.

Dal LakeMughal GardensHouseboatsSaffron Fields

Land of High Passes

Ladakh

Drive the world's highest motorable roads through Khardung La at 18,380 ft. Camp beside the colour-shifting Pangong Lake. Ride a double-humped camel in the sand dunes of Nubra Valley. Visit 1,000-year-old Buddhist monasteries perched on cliff edges and stargaze under some of the clearest skies on Earth.

Pangong LakeNubra ValleyKhardung LaMonasteries
5-10 Days May - Sep Explore →
Meadow of Flowers

Gulmarg

Ride the Gulmarg Gondola — the world's highest cable car — to 13,780 ft for panoramic Himalayan views. In winter, ski on powder snow across pristine slopes. In summer, trek through alpine meadows bursting with wildflowers, play on India's highest golf course, and horseback ride through pine forests.

Gondola RideSkiingGolf CourseMeadows
2-4 Days Year Round Explore →
Valley of Shepherds

Pahalgam

Walk through the stunning Betaab Valley made famous by Bollywood. Follow the crystal-clear Lidder River through Aru Valley to remote shepherd camps. Take pony rides through dense pine forests, fish for trout in glacial streams, and begin the sacred pilgrimage to the holy Amarnath Cave.

Betaab ValleyAru ValleyLidder RiverAmarnath Yatra
3-5 Days Mar - Oct Explore →
Meadow of Gold

Sonamarg

Trek to the breathtaking Thajiwas Glacier through fields of wildflowers. Camp beside the stunning Vishansar and Krishansar alpine lakes at 12,000 ft. Cross the legendary Zoji La pass into Ladakh. In winter, witness Sonamarg transform into a pristine white wonderland of untouched snow.

Thajiwas GlacierAlpine LakesZoji La PassCamping
2-4 Days Apr - Oct Explore →
Roof of the World

Nepal

Trek to Everest Base Camp through Sherpa villages and rhododendron forests. Explore the ancient temples and bustling markets of Kathmandu's Durbar Square. Paraglide over the stunning Phewa Lake in Pokhara. Discover Lumbini, the birthplace of Buddha, and spot Bengal tigers in Chitwan National Park.

Everest Base CampKathmanduPokharaChitwan
7-14 Days Oct - Apr Explore →
